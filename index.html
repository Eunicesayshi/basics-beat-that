You are playing a multi-player game called Beat That.

GAME RULES:
1. Start by entering the number of players who wish to play.
2. Players take turns to roll any number of dice entered at the start of each round.
3. The biggest possible number will automatically be generated from those digits.
4. The player with the biggest sum of all the numbers obtained thus far is the winner for that round!

HOW TO START:
Simply enter the number of players, then click the submit button.

TIP:
At any point of time, you can enter "reversed" to play the reversed mode where the smallest total sum wins. To change back to the normal mode thereafter, simply enter "normal".
