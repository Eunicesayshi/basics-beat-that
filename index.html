You are playing a 2-player game called Beat That.

GAME RULES:
1. Players take turns to roll any number of dice entered at the start of the round.
2. The biggest possible number will be automatically generated from those digits.
3. The player with the bigger sum of all the numbers obtained thus far is the winner for that round!

HOW TO START:
Simply get Player 1 to enter the number of dice the group wishes to roll for this round, then click the submit button.

TIP:
You can enter "reversed" to play the reversed mode where the smaller total sum wins. To change back to the normal mode thereafter, simply enter "normal".
